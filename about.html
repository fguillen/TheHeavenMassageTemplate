About me

Welcome, my name is Fernando (He/Him), and I am a passionate masseur. I am dedicated to researching and mastering techniques and traditions that promote deep relaxation and connection through conscious touch.

Giving massages has always come naturally to me, and I have been providing massages to friends and family for many years. Recently, I took my intuition to the next level by undergoing professional training, and I am eager to continue honing my skills through practice.

I consider myself a lifelong student and explorer of the art of massage, and I am excited to offer long, deep, and conscious relaxation massage sessions at a very affordable rate. I look forward to our next massage session together.
